How do you use BodyCheck Tech?

All you have to do is put the compression shirt on, turn on the vibration motor circuit, and start lifting. The progress page on the website will allow you to track your progress over time and determine if you overall have improved your form or not. In the Adjustments page you can increase the range of motion that best works for you. If you have any general questions the Resource page should be able to help with any questions about form or common mistakes.
