Why you should adjust your range of motion?

Adjusting the range of motion is a key step in customizing your workout experience. Every deadlifter lifts in a different fashion, and one deadlifters form may differ from anothers. Utilize the controls below to adjust the BodyCheck Techs sensivity to allow for a more fine tuned range of motion for your body.
